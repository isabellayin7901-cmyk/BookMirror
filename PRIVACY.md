# BookMirror 隐私政策 / Privacy Policy

_最后更新 / Last updated: 2026-05-30_

## 中文

BookMirror（“本应用”）是一款基于性格与星座的读书推荐工具。我们重视你的隐私。

**我们收集哪些信息**
- 你主动填写的：MBTI 类型、出生日期与出生地点（用于计算星盘）、阅读目标与偏好、性别（可选）、以及自由输入的需求文本。
- 这些信息**仅存储在你本机设备上**（应用本地存储），不会上传到我们的服务器长期保存。

**信息如何被使用**
- 为生成个性化推荐与画像，部分输入（如 MBTI、星座、需求文本）会在你触发分析时发送到我们的后端，并转发给 AI 服务商 Anthropic (Claude) 进行实时生成。
- 该过程为一次性调用，我们不会将其与你的身份关联存储。
- 出生日期/出生地点用于本地及服务端的星盘计算，不用于其他目的。

**第三方**
- AI 生成由 Anthropic 提供，受其隐私条款约束。
- 应用内的购书跳转会打开第三方 App / 网站（微信读书、淘宝、Apple Books 等），其行为受各自隐私政策约束。

**数据删除**
- 在「设置 → 清除所有数据」可一键删除本机全部数据。

**联系我们**
- 反馈或隐私问题请邮件：feedback@bookmirror.app

## English

BookMirror ("the App") is a personality- and astrology-based reading recommendation tool. We respect your privacy.

**What we collect**
- Information you provide: MBTI type, date and place of birth (for chart calculation), reading goals and preferences, gender (optional), and free-text needs.
- This data is **stored only on your device** (local storage) and is not retained on our servers.

**How it is used**
- To generate personalized recommendations and profiles, some inputs (e.g. MBTI, zodiac, needs text) are sent to our backend when you trigger an analysis, and forwarded to our AI provider Anthropic (Claude) for real-time generation.
- These are one-off calls; we do not store them linked to your identity.
- Birth date/place are used only for chart calculation.

**Third parties**
- AI generation is provided by Anthropic, subject to their privacy terms.
- In-app purchase links open third-party apps/sites (WeChat Read, Taobao, Apple Books, etc.) governed by their own policies.

**Data deletion**
- Use "Settings → Clear all data" to wipe all on-device data instantly.

**Contact**
- Feedback or privacy questions: feedback@bookmirror.app
